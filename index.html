About Your Trusted Travel Companion

FORLOK is a revolutionary travel collaboration platform that connects travelers going to the same destination, enabling them to share rides and split costs. Whether you're looking to share your journey or rent a vehicle, we make travel affordable, convenient, and eco-friendly.

Our platform supports both individual travelers and company owners, offering seamless pooling services for shared rides and comprehensive rental services for vehicles. Join thousands of users who are saving money while making new connections on their journeys.
