SOS Feature Your Safety Is Our Priority

FORLOK takes women safety seriously. Our SOS Feature is a one-tap emergency system built into every ride. Whether you're car pooling, bike pooling, or renting a vehicle, the SOS button is always accessible. In case of any emergency, one tap sends instant alerts to your pre-set emergency contacts with your real-time GPS location.

Our system also notifies nearby authorities and FORLOK's dedicated safety team who monitor the situation in real-time. The feature includes automatic audio recording during emergencies, a silent alert mode for discreet help, and integration with local police helplines. Every driver and rider on FORLOK is thoroughly verified with Aadhaar, driving license, and background checks.

How SOS Works

1. Tap the SOS button during any ride

2. Your live location is instantly shared with emergency contacts

4. Continuous monitoring until you confirm safety
